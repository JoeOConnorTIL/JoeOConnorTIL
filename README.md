# 👋 Hi, I'm Joe

I am a Data Consultant at **The Information Lab**. Having completed several placements focused on data analysis and visualisation, I am now transitioning into the earlier stages of the data process by shifting my focus to **Data Engineering**, where I am learning to build pipelines and work with cloud data warehouses.

Before transitioning into tech, I spent nine years as an officer in the Metropolitan Police, including five years in the Territorial Support Group. That background gave me a solid foundation in communication, handling pressure, and practical problem-solving, which heavily shapes how I approach technical challenges today.
